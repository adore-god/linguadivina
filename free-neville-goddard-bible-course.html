A Psychological Reading of Scripture

Free Self-Guided Course in Understanding Scripture

Discover the Bible in a new way with god.thway.uk — a free, self-guided course designed to teach you the key principles of reading and understanding scripture as a psychological and symbolic text. This pathway is built for anyone who senses there is more to the Bible than its surface reading offers, and who wants a structured, grounded way to explore it.

The approach here follows the line of those who read Scripture as a whole — not as isolated verses or disconnected historical episodes, but as a single, internally consistent system. The Bible is uniquely structured and carefully coded. Its foundational principles are introduced in Genesis and govern the entire narrative from beginning to end. Every story, name, pattern, and repetition grows from seeds planted at the start. Once you understand those seeds, the rest of the Bible opens.

This site works through that structure methodically — beginning with fundamental principles, then connecting you to cross-linked series collections that follow specific themes and passages across the full span of Scripture. The goal is not to tell you what to believe, but to equip you to read the text yourself, on its own terms, with increasing confidence and depth.

The Bible Interpreter Key

The site also features something new: the Lingua Divina Bible Interpreter Key — a free, structured linguistic framework built from Scripture's own internal logic.

The Bible's symbolic grammar has always been there. The Key makes it usable. It identifies the three foundational terms that govern the entire biblical narrative — YHVH/LORD (present consciousness), Ehyeh/I AM (assumed identity), and Elohim (the laws of creation that enforce whatever identity is occupied) — and shows how these three operate together as a coherent system across every book, story, and verse. Once you see the structure, you cannot unsee it. The same engine runs from Genesis to Revelation.

The practical application is direct: copy the Key and paste it into any AI alongside a Bible verse or passage, then ask for an interpretation using only the Key and the biblical narrative. The result is a psychological, symbolic reading of the text from within its own framework — not imposed from outside, but drawn out of the structure Scripture already contains. Any verse. Any passage. Any book.

For example:

"Give a detailed analysis of Genesis 2:24 using only this key and within the Bible narrative."

"Using only this key and the Bible, fully explain who Paul is in the context of 1 Corinthians 2, in everyday terms."

The Key is a companion to the course — not a replacement for it. The articles and collections on this site show the Key's principles applied across dozens of passages, giving you worked examples before you begin interpreting independently. Start with the fundamentals, read the interpretations, then take the Key into your own study. That sequence is the most direct route from unfamiliarity to genuine interpretive confidence.

Start with the Fundamentals

The course begins in the sidebar with the essential principles needed to unlock Scripture. These are not abstract theological concepts — they are the operational rules the biblical text itself follows, grounded in Genesis and traceable through every subsequent book. You will learn to recognise the symbolic meaning behind biblical characters, events, names, and promises; to understand how faith and assumption function within the narrative as precise creative mechanics; and to read the Bible as a map of states of consciousness rather than as external history.

From the fundamentals, the series collections take you deeper — each collection linked to specific passages and themes, each article cross-referenced so that connections across the text become visible. Scripture interprets Scripture. The Key makes the mechanism explicit.

A Free, Flexible Learning Experience

This course is completely free and entirely self-paced. There are no sign-ups, no deadlines, and no prescribed order beyond the recommendation to begin with the fundamentals before moving into the collections. You decide the pace and direction. The material is here when you are ready for it.

The combination of foundational principles, worked interpretations, and the Lingua Divina Key gives you three layers of access to the same text: the concepts explained, the concepts demonstrated, and the concepts made into a tool you can use yourself on any passage you encounter. Most people who engage with all three find that their reading of the Bible changes permanently — not because the text has changed, but because they now have the structural vocabulary to hear what it has always been saying.

Begin Your Journey

Start with the fundamental principles in the sidebar. From there, the series collections will take you through the major themes of Scripture with that foundation in place. And when you are ready to interpret independently, the Bible Interpreter Key is there as your companion tool — free, precise, and built entirely from within the Bible's own structure.

The Bible has always contained this depth. This course exists to help you find it.
